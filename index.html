Classes

Spinning

Spinning saddle up and experience the most enjoyable and effective workouts ever. Our spinning classes are ideal for people of all levels of fitness and experience, weather you are new to exercise or a regular gym junkie!

TRX

This class will push you to new levels of strength using trx suspension training bands and your own body weight. TRX uses body weight exercises which develops strength, balance, flexibility and core stability simultaneously.

Bodypump

Is one of the best ways to tone up and get in shape as it challenges all of your major muscle groups while you squat, press, lift and curl. This is a resistance based workout using bars & weights to burn calories even after you've left the class!

Bootcamp

This circuit training based class is designed to challenge your body and mind in a fun environment, all the while building strength and fitness through a variety of exercise stations targeting fitness and individual body parts.

Bums & Tums

You cannot beat this class for targeting those “hard to handle” body parts that need a little bit more attention! Tone and firm up your tummy, hips, thighs and bum with this brilliant workout dedicated to ‘bums n tums’!

Pilates

This mat pilates class works to balance all muscle groups' strength and flexibility, with an emphasis on challenging the core muscles with each movement. Mat pilates is a challenging yet safe method to sculpt your body and to feel increased agility in your every day movements.
